About Me

Thanks for checking out my website! I'm a senior graduate student in the Goodrich and Kugel lab at CU Boulder. I joined the lab in May of 2016 and have been working on two main projects:

  1. Understanding Pol II transcriptional regulation upon HSV-1 infection
  2. Investigating the lifecycle and metabolism of B2 ncRNA
